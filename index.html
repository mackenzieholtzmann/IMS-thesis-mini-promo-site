A NEW WAY TO WAKE UP

[ABOUT]

[ARRAY]ALARM was created for the people who can't wake up with just one alarm in the morning. If you're someone who needs 3 (ok 10) alarms to get out of bed - then you know how tiresome it is to individually set them each night. With [ARRAY]ALARM, instead of setting 10 seperate alarms - you can create groups of multiple alarms. Inspired by minimalist design and beautiful visuals, we make waking up enjoyable again.

[FEATURES]

MULTIPLE ALARMS

[MULTIPLE ALARMS]
We all know you can't get out of bed with just one alarm. Set as many as you like - and organize them into easy to use groups.

BEAUTIFUL VISUALS

[BEAUTIFUL VISUALS]
Why wake up to a blank screen when you could wake up to beautiful works of art? You'll get a different visual for each alarm in the group.

AND MUCH MORE

[AND MUCH MORE]
Choose how you snooze. Slide or shake to sleep a little longer.

Customizable sounds to wake up to - including music from your own library.

Track your habits. View a list of stats right from the main screen.

[HOW IT WORKS]

[ARRAY]ALARM was created using a mean stack development approach. It also utilizes Ionic V1, HTML5 and CSS3.

[DEVELOPER]

My name is Mackenzie Holtzmann and I am the creator of [ARRAY]ALARM. I currently enrolled in the Interactive Media Specialist Program at Fanshawe College. Prior to enrolling in IMS, I completed Fanshawe's 3 year advanced diploma in Graphic Design. This unique background allows me to write highly efficient code, while implementing my strong sense of design. The inspiration behind [ARRAY]ALARM was my personal struggle to wake up in the morning. I needed a better way to organize my never ending list of alarms, and thus, [ARRAY]ALARM was born. I programmed the [ARRAY]ALARM to be reliable and efficient, and designed it to be user friendly and visually appealing.
